weight: 1
---

* **Joomla! 5.4.x, 6.x.x**: CM Gift Card is only compatible with Joomla! 5.4.x and newer, include 6.x.x. Any older versions of Joomla!, for example Joomla! 3.9.x, is not supported. Please check [Joomla!'s Technical Requirements](https://docs.joomla.org/) for more information.
* **Bootstrap 5**: CM Gift Card is compatible with both Bootstrap 5 and requires one of these versions available on your site.
* **Cron job**: You need cron job to send electronic gift cards on their specific delivery dates.
* **mPDF library**: You need mPDF library if you want to send PDF invoice to customer.
