When purchase a gift card, customer can pick a design for his/her gift card. You can create gift card designs by navigating to CM Gift Card's `Gift Card Designs` section.

![](/images/design_01.jpg)

To create a new design, you click "New" button on the toolbar.

![](/images/design_02.jpg)

* **Title**: The title of your gift card design.
* **Type**: The design is for physical gift card or electronic gift card.
* **Image**: The thumbnail image of the design.
* **Status**: Only published designs are shown in front-end.

After creating your gift card design, you can see it in the design list. You can sort the designs by using the icons in the first column of the list.
